--- title: Countdown to version 1.0 author: Stian Grenborgen ---

We have scheduled a date for the long awaited 1.0 release: January, 2nd 2023! This will mark the twenty year anniversary for FreeCol's first official release.

However, we will still be releasing new versions regurlarly with our changes rather than saving them for the final release. This will allow all the changes to be tested properly, and might allow more people to contribute towards the goal of making the 1.0 release truely great.

It's just one and a half month since our last release, but we are still close to releasing a new version with several exciting changes like in-game music and a new animated river graphics.

Perhaps you could help as well?
